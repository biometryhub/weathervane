Easily navigate and retrieve weather datasets for anywhere in Australia!

Simply specify a latitude and longitude (by entering the numbers directly or by choosing a location on the interactive map), and a date range of interest, and click 'Update Coordinates/Date Range' to start the download. You can use the Variables view pane to see a preview of the time series data available for each variable and include/exclude them using the switches on the left-hand side. When ready, clck the 'Download data to CSV...' button to retrieve your specified weather variables in a convenient spreadsheet.
