Referencing

The weather datasets currently retrieved are curated by SILO, who make them available under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International Licence. Their data is in turn sourced from the Australian Bureau of Meteorology and their weather stations.

Please reference them appropriately in any publications or other research outputs. See for example:

  • https://www.longpaddock.qld.gov.au/silo/about/access-data/
  • http://www.bom.gov.au/other/copyright.shtml

Credits

The weathervane App and ancillary codes were developed by Russell Edson and Sam Rogers to aid researchers in retrieving weather data to incorporate into statistical analyses of agronomic experiments and plant-breeding trials.
